Joshua Loerker

Joshua Loerker is a Digital Media specialist who partners with businesses, creators, and organizations to deliver their message and reach their target audience through technology. He knows what it takes to bridge the gap between technology and creativity to create an effective product.

Joshua has worked at Brookhaven National Laboratory, supporting employees with on-site technical issues, from laptops to printers, and more.

He also runs a live broadcast every week on Amazon's Twitch.tv platform. With over two million active broadcasters, Joshua is one of just over seventeen thousand broadcasters that is partnered with Twitch.tv. In addition, he regularly collaborates with creators, and other renowned companies in the digital media world such as Discord, an online voice application that hosts over eighty-seven million users.

Joshua is currently studying Information Systems at Stony Brook University, with a specialization in Digital Media.

In his free time, he loves playing sports with his friends, playing video games like Mario Kart, and exploring nature.
